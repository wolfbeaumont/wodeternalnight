A

Amria A Daennosi curse or violent oath. Taken from Amriat, the name of the vampire in the First City who forced Daenna, the mother of all Daennos to abandon her husband and home.

Anarch Movement Ad-hoc groups of vampires, that refuse to join a particular sect, and so establish an anarch state in their domain.

Angustri A Daennosi ring, though typically one with magical properties.

Assamites A vampire clan of assassins, with a penchant for blood rituals.

Autarkis A vampire divorced from vampiric society, either unnoticed or too powerful to act against.

B

Baba "Old woman". A Daennosi term of respect..

Bater A Daennosi term meaning "so be it", typically used to conclude a deal or arrangement of some kind.

Baxt A Daennosi expression for "good luck".

Brujah A vampire clan of warrior philosophers turned rebels and criminals, ready to frenzy at a moment's notice.

Bujo An elaborate Daennosi con on a gaje.

C

Cainite A common term for vampire amoungst their own kind.

Caitiff Clanless vampires, orphans of vampiric society.

Camarilla A sect of genteel vampires that seek to co-exist with mortals, hidden amongst them.

D

Dae Daennosi word for Mother.

Daebara Daennosi word for the female head of a local family.

Daebaro Daennosi word for the male head of a local family.

Deltumnimos A magical Daennosi potion that expands the abilities of the mind, literally meaning "God's embrace".

Dhartime A Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of Earth.

Diklo A heavy Daennosi scarf, typically weighted and used as a weapon.

Dilo A Daennosi word for a fool or imbecile.

Dook a Daennosi word for the Sight, or magic in general.

Draba Daennosi Items of magical power, typically amulets.

Drabarne Daennosi mages skilled at creating draba.

Dukkerin The Daennosi art of fortune-telling or using the Sight.

E

F

Fetter A place, person or object that ties a spirit to the shadowlands and prevents them from moving on.

Followers of Set A vampire clan that worships a chthotic god and seeks out secret places, protecting ancient artefacts.

G

Gaje A non-Daennosi human.

Gangrel A beastial vampire clan, that prefers the wilds over cities.

Giorgio A non-Daennosi in general.

Giovanni An insular vampire clan who practice necromancy and have a talent for global finances.

H

I

Inconnu An enigmatic vampire sect dedicated towards reaching Golconda.

J

Jastima A Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of water.

Jhanaki A Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of air.

K

Kindred Meaning vampire, this term is typically only used by vampires belonging to the Camarilla, as a sign of fellowship.

Kuyan A Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of fire.

L

Lasombra A proud vampire clan that commands the shadows and darkness itself.

M

Malkavians A vampire clan fractured by madness, each suffering from a form of insanity.

Marhime Unclean or impure. Those who have defiled their blood. The opposite of wuzho.

Mule A ghost or spirit of the dead.

Mule-vi A medium or object capable of reaching the world of the dead.

N

Nosferatu A hidious vampire clan that affords the mortal world, living underground in places like sewers where they traffic in secrets.

O

P

Paramitsha Daennosi fairy tales.

Patteran The special Blood Affinity of the Phuri Dae, or an important symbol or other piece of information.

Pena Sister.

Phral A full-blooded Daennos or true Daennosi.

Phuri Dae One of the Daennosi families of power. Also any wise woman or woman with magical powers.

Posta A sacrifice, usually ritual in nature.

Prala Brother.

Prikata Bad luck.

Q

R

Ravnos A nomadic vampire clan that is tied to the Daennos, with a talent for illusions and vices.

S

Sabbat A sect of vampires dedicated to destroying the ancients, with no regard for mortals.

Satarma Star. Also a Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of spirit.

Shilmulo A vampire. One of the "cold dead".

T

Talith A Daennos weapon consisting of a heavy scarf (diklo) weighted and barbed along the end, or the art of using such a weapon.

Tal'Mahe'Ra A mythic vampire sect, that spends more in the Underworld than amongst the living.

Toreador A vampire clan that lives for sensual pleasure and beauty.

Tremere A vampire clan with a scorcerous past and a penchant for blood magic.

Tsurara A new and radical family of power.

Tumnimos The Daennosi term for the vampiric embrace.

Tzimisce An eldrich vampire clan of the Old World, with little in common with mortals, they manipulate flesh and bones at whim.

U

V

Vardo A Daennos wagon, RV camper etc.

Ventrue An entitled vampire clan that believe in the creed of greed and power.

W

Wuzho Pure, untainted. The opposite of marhime.

Z

Zapaderin A unique, hypnotising Daennos dance form and Blood Affinity.
