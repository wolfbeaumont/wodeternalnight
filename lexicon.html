A

Amria (Ahm-REE-ah)
A Daennosi curse or violent oath. Taken from Amriat, the name of the vampire in the First City who forced Daenna, the mother of all Daennos to abandon her husband and home.

Angustri (angh-GOOS-tree)
A Daennosi ring, though typically one with magical properties.

B

Baba (BAH-ba)
"Old woman". A Daennosi term of respect..

Bater (BAH-ter)
A Daennosi term meaning "so be it", typically used to conclude a deal or arrangement of some kind.

Baxt (BAX-tuh)
A Daennosi expression for "good luck".

Bujo (BOO-zhou)
An elaborate Daennosi con on a gaje.

C

D

Dae (Dah-EE)
Daennosi word for Mother.

Daebara (Dah-bar-RAH)
Daennosi word for the female head of a local family.

Daebaro (Dah-bar-OH)
Daennosi word for the male head of a local family.

Deltumnimos (del-TOOHM-nee-mos)
A magical Daennosi potion that expands the abilities of the mind, literally meaning "God's embrace".

Dhartime (DHAR-ti-mey)
A Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of Earth.

Diklo (DEEK-loh)
A heavy Daennosi scarf, typically weighted and used as a weapon.

Dilo (DEE-loh)
A Daennosi word for a fool or imbecile.

Dook (Dook)
a Daennosi word for the Sight, or magic in general.

Draba (DRAH-bah)
Daennosi Items of magical power, typically amulets.

Drabarne (drah-BAR-ney)
Daennosi mages skilled at creating draba.

Dukkerin (DOOK-er-in)
The Daennosi art of fortune-telling or using the Sight.

E

F

Fetter (Fe-TER)
A place, person or object that ties a spirit to the shadowlands and prevents them from moving on.

G

Gaje (GAH-zhey)
A non-Daennosi human.

Giorgio (JOOR-gee-oh)
A non-Daennosi in general.

J

Jastima (ZSHAH-tsee-mah)
A Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of water.

Jhanaki (ZSHAH-nah-kee)
A Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of air.

K

Kuyan (KOO-yahn)
A Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of fire.

L

M

Marhime (MAH-ree-mey)
Unclean or impure. Those who have defiled their blood. The opposite of wuzho.

Mule (MOOH-ley)
A ghost or spirit of the dead.

Mule-vi (MOOH-ley-vee)
A medium or object capable of reaching the world of the dead.

P

Paramitsha (pah-rah-MEESH-tah)
Daennosi fairy tales.

Patteran (PAH-tehr-an)
The special Blood Affinity of the Phuri Dae, or an important symbol or other piece of information.

Pena (PEEN-ah)
Sister.

Phral (Frahl)
A full-blooded Daennos or true Daennosi.

Phuri Dae (FOO-ree dae-EE)
One of the Daennosi families of power. Also any wise woman or woman with magical powers.

Posta (POH-sta)
A sacrifice, usually ritual in nature.

Prala (PRAH-lah)
Brother.

Prikata (PREEK-ka-za)
Bad luck.

S

Satarma (sah-TAHR-ma)
Star. Also a Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of spirit.

Shilmulo (SHEEL-moo-loh)
A vampire. One of the "cold dead".

T

Talith (TAH-leth)
A Daennos weapon consisting of a heavy scarf (diklo) weighted and barbed along the end, or the art of using such a weapon.

Tsurara (TSOO-rah-rah)
A new and radical family of power.

Tumnimos (TOOM-nee-mos)
The vampiric embrace.

Vardo (VAR-doh)
A Daennos wagon, RV camper etc.

W

Wuzho (WOO-zhoh)
Pure, untainted. The opposite of marhime.
