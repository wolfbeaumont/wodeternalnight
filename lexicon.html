A

Alastor
These are secret agents of the Inner Circle, immune from prosecution by Princes for their actions as they spy on local cities and hunt criminals from the 'Red List'.

Amaranth
Another term for vampiric diablerie, draining another's vitae and very soul for their power.

Amria
A Daennosi curse or violent oath. Taken from Amriat, the name of the vampire in the First City who forced Daenna, the mother of all Daennos to abandon her husband and home.

Anarch Movement
Ad-hoc groups of vampires, that refuse to join a particular sect, and so establish an anarch state in their domain.

Angustri
A Daennosi ring, though typically one with magical properties.

Archbishop
The vampiric ruler of a domain aligned with the Sabbat, they answer to the Cardinals and Regent.

Archon
Envoys of the Justicars, to enact their will.

Assamites
A vampire clan of assassins, with a penchant for blood rituals.

Autarkis
A vampire divorced from vampiric society, either unnoticed or too powerful to act against.

B

Baba
"Old woman". A Daennosi term of respect.

Baron
The vampiric ruler of an Anarch city.

Bater
A Daennosi term meaning "so be it", typically used to conclude a deal or arrangement of some kind.

Baxt
A Daennosi expression for "good luck".

Bishop
Servants of a domain's Archbishop, they are either in charge of a specific aspect of Cainite life, or are a more general spiritual leader. Some Sabbat domains have councils of Bishops instead of Archbishops.

Book of Nod
Essentially, the vampiric bible, it chronicles the story of Caine, the first of their kind, after he was turned into a vampire. Only fragments remain, more myth than fact.

Brujah
A vampire clan of warrior philosophers turned rebels and criminals, ready to frenzy at a moment's notice.

Bujo
An elaborate Daennosi con on a gaje.

C

Cainite
A common term for vampire amoungst their own kind.

Caitiff
Clanless vampires, orphans of vampiric society.

Camarilla
A sect of genteel vampires that seek to co-exist with mortals, hidden amongst them.

Cardinal
Sabbat leaders of massive geographic regions, they answer only to the Regent. They are responsible for directing the Great Jyhad.

Childe
The progeny of a vampire.

D

Dae
Daennosi word for Mother.

Daebara
Daennosi word for the female head of a local family.

Daebaro
Daennosi word for the male head of a local family.

Deltumnimos
A magical Daennosi potion that expands the abilities of the mind, literally meaning "God's embrace".

Dhartime
A Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of Earth.

Diablerie
The act of exsanguinating another vampire to the point of True Death, shunned by some sects and encouraged by others.

Diklo
A heavy Daennosi scarf, typically weighted and used as a weapon.

Dilo
A Daennosi word for a fool or imbecile.

Dook
A Daennosi word for the Sight, or magic in general.

Draba
Daennosi items of magical power, typically amulets.

Drabarne
Daennosi mages skilled at creating draba.

Ductus
Sabbat pack leaders (plural ducti), an honorific title for what is essentially a gang leader.

Dukkerin
The Daennosi art of fortune-telling or using the Sight.

E

F

Fetter
A place, person or object that ties a spirit to the shadowlands and prevents them from moving on.

Followers of Set
A vampire clan that worships a chthotic god and seeks out secret places, protecting ancient artefacts.

G

Gaje
A non-Daennosi human.

Gangrel
A beastial vampire clan, that prefers the wilds over cities.

Generation
Usually used to refer to how far removed a vampire is from Caine himself, by number of generations, with vampires growing weaker with each higher generation.

Giorgio
A non-Daennosi in general.

Giovanni
An insular vampire clan who practice necromancy and have a talent for global finances.

H

Harpy
These are vampires within the Camarilla who set trends in various tastes and topics.

Hound
They work for Sheriffs within the Camarilla, appointed by either the Sheriff or Prince.

I

Inconnu
An enigmatic vampire sect dedicated towards reaching Golconda.

Inner Circle
Each clan within the Camarilla has a member on the Inner Circle (usually the clan leader), where they meet once every thirteen years to guide the Camarilla's future.

J

Jastima
A Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of water.

Jhanaki
A Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of air.

Justicar
Judges appointed by the Inner Circle, they serve for terms of thirteen years and may only be challenged by another Justicar. They enforce the Traditions when breached on a larger scale and are the Inner Circles agents and enforcers of their will.

K

Keeper of Elysium
This honorific title of the Camarilla is given to those charged with upholding the customs of Elysium, taking care of the sites for their Princes.

Kindred
Meaning vampire, this term is typically only used by vampires belonging to the Camarilla, as a sign of fellowship.

Kuyan
A Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of fire.

L

Lasombra
A proud vampire clan that commands the shadows and darkness itself.

Lextalionis
The Blood Hunt, this is vampiric decree by Princes for the foulest of crimes, forfeiting the intended's life should they be captured. It is used exceedingly rarely. Diablerie is also pardoned when performed on the outcast.

M

Malkavians
A vampire clan fractured by madness, each suffering from a form of insanity.

Marhime
Daennosi term for "unclean or impure". Those who have defiled their blood. The opposite of wuzho.

Mule
Daennosi term for a ghost or spirit of the dead.

Mule-vi
Daennosi term for a medium or object capable of reaching the world of the dead.

N

Nosferatu
A hidious vampire clan that affords the mortal world, living underground in places like sewers where they traffic in secrets.

O

P

Pack Priest
Each Sabbat pack has a priest in charge of their spiritual needs, second-in-command to the Ductus, they officiate and create rituals for their pack. All packs have at least one pack priest.

Paladin
Handpicked Templars, chosen to senior the most important members of the Sabbat.

Paramitsha
Daennosi fairy tales.

Patteran
The special Blood Affinity of the Daennosi Phuri Dae family, or an important symbol or other piece of information for them.

Pena
Daennosi term for "Sister".

Phral
A full-blooded Daennos or true Daennosi.

Phuri Dae
One of the Daennosi families of power. Also any wise woman or woman with magical powers.

Posta
Daennosi term for a sacrifice, usually ritual in nature.

Prala
Daennosi term for "Brother".

Priscus
These are unofficial advisors (plural prisci) to the local Sabbat authorities.

Prikata
Daennosi term for "Bad luck".

Primogen
A title for either members of a council that advise the local Prince or simply the eldest and most influencial vampires of the local domain.

Prince
The vampiric ruler of a city aligned with the Camarilla.

Q

R

Ravnos
A nomadic vampire clan that is tied to the Daennos, with a talent for illusions and vices.

Regent
The sovereign ruler of the Sabbat sect from their capital in Mexico City.

S

Sabbat
A sect of vampires dedicated to destroying the ancients, with no regard for mortals.

Satarma
Daennosi term for a star. Also a Daennosi touched by, or "claimed by" the element of spirit.

Scourge
A role within Camarilla whose responsiblity is to purge those of 14th generation or higher, typically in cities infested with thin-bloods.

Sheriff
The right hand of a Prince, they are appointed to oversee the enforcement of the Prince's will within a city.

Shilmulo
Daennosi term for "vampire". One of the "cold dead".

Sire
The one who embraced a vampire, and is essentially their parent.

T

Talith
A Daennos weapon consisting of a heavy scarf (diklo) weighted and barbed along the end, or the art of using such a weapon.

Tal'Mahe'Ra
A mythic vampire sect, that spends more in the Underworld than amongst the living.

Templar
Elite bodyguards, appointed to protect senior Sabbat figures, as well as serve them in other duties.

Toreador
A vampire clan that lives for sensual pleasure and beauty.

Tremere
A vampire clan with a scorcerous past and a penchant for blood magic.

Tsurara
A new and radical Daennosi family of power.

Tumnimos
The Daennosi term for the vampiric embrace.

Tzimisce
An eldrich vampire clan of the Old World, with little in common with mortals, they manipulate flesh and bones at whim.

U

V

Vardo
A Daennos wagon, RV camper etc.

Ventrue
An entitled vampire clan that believe in the creed of greed and power.

W

Wuzho
Daennosi term for "pure or untainted". The opposite of marhime.

X

Y

Z

Zapaderin
A unique, hypnotising Daennos dance form and Blood Affinity.
