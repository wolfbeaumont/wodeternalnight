Daennosi Backgrounds


Talismans

You possess one or more magical talismans, or draba as the Daennos call them, which only they can fashion, if skilled. Whether you "acquired" one or had them made by a drabarne, these items contain powerful magic, however they do not work for the dead and may only work for the undead if fashioned by a Ravnos drabarne.
The greater your score in this Background, the more valuable the talisman you possess. You may possess more than one talisman, dividing your score amongst the draba you possess.

●           Novice: You possess talismans worth one level of draba.
●●         Practised: You possess talismans worth two levels of draba.
●●●       Competent: You possess talismans worth three levels of draba.
●●●●     Expert: You possess talismans worth four levels of draba.
●●●●●   Master: You possess talismans worth five levels of draba.
