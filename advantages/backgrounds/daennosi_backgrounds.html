1.1 Allies

Allies are typically family members or more uncommonly Daennos in other families. It is rare for allies to willingly risk themselves for the character unless they are close kin.

1.2 Contacts

Typically gaje in a city's underworld or influential Daennos, particularly elders with powerful magic.

1.3 Fame

It gains the respect of the Daennos but puts them on the radar of gaje police etc. or it can represent the character as an entertainer.

1.4 Resources

Funds typically come from non-mainstream sources, though the Daennos don't typically hold on to wealth or possessions.
